CEO'S MESSAGE

Tariq Abu Jbarah Investments focuses on expanding our brands through franchising, as we work with investors and operators in key markets to bring the heart and flavours of each concept to people everywhere. Each brand follows a specific expansion plan, based on, market adaptability and maturity, the concept niche and thorough analysis. The key to successful franchising programs aligning with franchise partners who share our values and vision, as we provide them with the knowledge, formulas, training and ongoing support to ensure that their development plans and businesses are successful. Tariq Abu Jbarah takes a serious approach to our training and support program, as one of the pillars of franchisee success. Our open communication and hands-on support policies have been key for this program and our strategy. As we continue to expand our portfolio of brands, only specific brands that meet the franchising criteria are graduated into our franchise program. Currently, Operation: Falafel, our MiddleEastern street food brand, is seeing a healthy expansion rate, as we continue to develop the GCC market and bring on new markets in Europe, Asia and North America. Awani, our successful Levant cuisine casual dining concept has opportunities available in the GCC. For more information, please email us at franchising@tariqabujbarah.com
